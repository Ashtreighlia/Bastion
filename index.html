Welcome to Bastion

Bastion is a fresh, faster-paced spin on the Commander format for Magic: The Gathering.

In this variant, players harness three powerful spells from their Bastion, which synergize seamlessly with their deck. Designed for quick, high-impact games, Bastion captures the strategic depth of Commander in a more time-efficient format. Enter a realm where classic spells meet fresh strategic possibilities, and each game unfolds a unique story shaped by your Champion's leadership, your Relic's enduring power, and your Pact's tactical mastery. With streamlined 50-card decks and focused gameplay, every decision becomes crucial in these fast-paced encounters.
